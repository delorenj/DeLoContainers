STEP 1: PHYSICAL RESET (DO THIS NOW)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Go to your Synology NAS (physical device)
2. Locate the RESET button (usually on back panel, might need paperclip)
3. With NAS powered ON and running:
   - Press and HOLD reset button
   - Count: "One Mississippi, Two Mississippi, Three Mississippi, Four Mississippi"
   - Release when you hear ONE BEEP (about 4 seconds)
   - If no beep, release at 4 seconds anyway

4. WAIT 2-3 minutes for reset to complete
   - Power LED will blink during process
   - Network activity will resume when ready
